02 The Place

A retreat anchored in the Western Ghats.

Tea gardens, milky waterfalls and the cool breath of Kerala’s old British plantation town.

Munnar is well known for its milky waterfalls, tea and spices plantations, lakes and Dams. Getting there is half-the-Fun when one cannot describe the loveliness of this beautiful hill plantations in plain words. Walk through the tea gardens and spend your vacation in tidy and fresh surroundings with fine weather and natural beauty.

Munnar is especially known for the Neelakurinji flowers that bloom once in 12 years in Rajamala turning it into a hill of blue flowers. Besides nature’s charm, Munnar is an ideal destination for active sports including trekking, boating, horse riding, elephant riding, fishing and paragliding. Situated 1700 metres above sea level, Munnar is an old British tea-plantation town nestled among the Western Ghats just 120 Kms from Cochin.
